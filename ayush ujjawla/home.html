FAQs - Ujjwala Ayush Start-Up Portal

FAQs - Ujjwala Ayush Start-Up Portal

Q1: Do I need to compulsorily form a company before registering?
No, it is not compulsory to form a company before registering. You can register as an individual or a group with a business idea in the AYUSH sector.
Q2: Can I submit more than one application?
Yes, you can submit multiple applications if you have different business ideas. However, each application will be evaluated separately based on its own merits.
Q3: Are private investors also incubating start-ups through this portal?
Yes, private investors and venture capitalists can also incubate start-ups through the portal, in addition to government and AYUSH-related organizations.
Q4: Is the registration process online or offline?
The entire process is online, from registration to submission of applications and communication with the portal's incubators.
